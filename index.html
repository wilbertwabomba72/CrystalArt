Offline Puzzle Experience

Crystal Art:
Design & Relax

Crystal Art: Design & Relax is a calm, color-rich puzzle game where each level asks you to sort and place gems in the correct order to reveal a recognizable picture. Every stage is fully playable offline and designed for short, satisfying sessions on iPhone and iPad.

What Makes It Special

Offline by Design

No network is required to play. Progress, stars, and settings are stored locally on the device.

50 Hand-Tuned Stages

Each puzzle uses a recognizable gem pattern with a carefully paced difficulty curve and star goals.

Relaxed Visual Style

Soft candy colors, gemstone palettes, ambient music, and clean layouts create a cozy puzzle atmosphere.

Simple, Tactile Play

Select a gem container, move pieces between columns, and place the correct gem to complete the picture.

Gameplay Overview

The goal is to reveal the final artwork one gem at a time. Each puzzle starts with mixed gem stacks. You can move gems between storage columns, but stacking rules matter, so every move should help build the solution. A highlighted board tile shows the next required gem color, and efficient play earns more stars.

  • Portrait-only layout optimized for iOS 13 and later.
  • Supports music, sound effects, haptics, and handedness preference.
  • Includes level select, stars, progress saving, and replay-friendly difficulty.
