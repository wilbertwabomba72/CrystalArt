Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Crystal Art: Design & Relax handles information when you use the game. Crystal Art: Design & Relax is designed as a standalone offline game and does not require account creation or online gameplay.

1. Information We Collect

The game does not intentionally collect personal information from users through the gameplay experience. You can play the game without creating an account, signing in, or providing personal profile information.

2. Local Device Data

The game may store gameplay-related information locally on your device, such as:

  • Level progress
  • Stars earned
  • Checkpoint data
  • Audio, haptics, and handedness preferences

This information is used only to support the in-game experience and is kept on the device unless you remove the app or clear the saved data.

3. Network Use

Crystal Art: Design & Relax is built as an offline game. It does not require internet access to play and does not depend on remote servers for normal gameplay.

4. Analytics, Tracking, and Advertising

The game does not include third-party advertising SDKs, behavioral tracking, or in-app analytics services as part of its core offline gameplay design.

5. Purchases and Accounts

The game does not require a user account to access gameplay. If platform-level features are added in the future, this Privacy Policy may be updated accordingly.

6. Children’s Privacy

The game is intended to provide a safe puzzle experience. No personal information is knowingly collected from children through the app itself.
